about

Hi there! My name is Esperanza (aka Banza) and I'm the owner & operator of Banza's Bakery! Baking has always been a passion of mine and in June of 2017 I took a leap of faith and started marketing my creations. What you see today has been years in the making and I'm super excited to see how it will continue to grow! I didn't want to just start a business, I wanted to do it with a purpose. In the words of one of my favorite role models in history, Malcolm X, "A man [or woman] who stands for nothing will fall for anything." To give back to my community and to promote initiatives I identify with, I've pledged to donate 10% of ALL quarterly profits to organizations addressing the needs of the black and brown communities. Check out the rest of my website or my Instragram (@banzasbakery) to see the organizations I've donated to, and find out how you can donate yourself! In addition, I offer vegan options for all of my cakes and cupcakes. So you can enjoy my creations knowing you're making the best choice for animals and the environment. Thank you for visiting my website and I hope to work with you soon!
