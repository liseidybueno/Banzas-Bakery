Hi there! Please fill out this order form if you're interested in placing an order. At least two weeks advance notice is preferred, less notice will depend on availability of items and will be considered on a case-by-case basis. All orders can be picked up in Norwood, Bronx. Delivery option is available depending on availability and distance, please indicate if you're interested in this option below. All orders over $100 require a 20% deposit. An email confirming your order will be sent with deposit instructions, the final order price, and delivery availability/fee within 24 hours.

10% of ALL quarterly profits will be donated to organizations addressing the needs of the black and brown communities! Check out my Instragram (@banzasbakery) to see the organizations I've donated to, and find out how you can donate yourself!

Feel free to submit a request using my contact form if you have any questions!
